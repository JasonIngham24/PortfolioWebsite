# Personal Portfolio Website

This repository contains my personal portfolio website—a central hub showcasing who I am, the work I've done, and how to get in touch. Built to highlight both my academic journey and personal projects, this site serves as an interactive résumé and introduction to my professional identity.

## Home Page

A clean landing page featuring a brief introduction and snapshot of my background.

## Projects

A collection of the projects I've completed throughout my academic career as well as personal ventures. Each project includes descriptions, key features, and relevant links.

## About Me

A more personal section exploring my interests, values, and the things I enjoy (and don’t enjoy!) outside of coding and coursework.

## Résumé

A downloadable and easy-to-read copy of my most up-to-date résumé.

## Contact Me

A simple and direct way to get in touch—featuring links to email, social platforms, or other communication channels.
